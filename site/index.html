Mathematical Framework

The framework is built on rigorous mathematical foundations. For example, the scalar field governing equation takes the form:

$\Box \phi + V'(\phi) + \xi R \phi = 0$

where $\phi$ is the scalar field, $\Box$ is the d'Alembert operator, $V(\phi)$ is the potential, $R$ is the Ricci scalar, and $\xi$ is the coupling constant.

The exceptional Lie group E$_8$ root lattice provides the symmetry structure:

$\text{E}_8 \subset \text{SO}(16)$

With 240 root vectors forming the vertices of an 8-dimensional polytope. The GEM field tensor unifying gravity and electromagnetism is:

$F_{\mu\nu} = \partial_\mu A_\nu - \partial_\nu A_\mu + \frac{\kappa}{2}(\partial_\mu h_\nu - \partial_\nu h_\mu)$

where $A_\mu$ is the electromagnetic potential, $h_\mu$ is the gravitoelectric potential, and $\kappa$ is the gravitational coupling.
