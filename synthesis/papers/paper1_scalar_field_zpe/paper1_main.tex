% Abstract
\begin{abstract}
\noindent
We present a comprehensive pedagogical treatment of scalar field theory and its coupling to the quantum vacuum zero-point energy (ZPE). Beginning with mathematical preliminaries in tensor calculus and differential geometry, we develop the Klein-Gordon Lagrangian formulation for scalar fields and explore vacuum fluctuation phenomena including the Casimir effect. We then investigate parametric resonance mechanisms for coherent field-vacuum coupling, with applications to energy extraction proposals and experimental tests.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{Key Topics}: Scalar field dynamics • Zero-point energy • Casimir effect • Quantum vacuum • Parametric resonance • Field-vacuum coupling

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{Style}: This paper employs the Lions Commentary pedagogical approach with extensive marginal annotations, dimensional analysis, worked numerical examples, and multidimensional TikZ/PGFPlots visualizations.
\end{abstract}
