% Abstract
\begin{abstract}
\noindent
We present a comprehensive pedagogical treatment of gravitational-electromagnetic unification through scalar field intermediaries. Beginning with a historical overview of unification attempts from Einstein to modern scalar-tensor theories, we develop the theoretical framework for coupling gravity and electromagnetism through scalar mediators. We explore scalar-tensor formulations of general relativity, investigate mechanisms for electromagnetic-gravitational field coupling, and derive unified equations of motion for the coupled system. The treatment includes dimensional analysis, solution techniques for unified field equations, and experimental signatures of gravitational-electromagnetic coupling in various regimes.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{Key Topics}: Scalar-tensor gravity • Electromagnetic-gravitational coupling • Unified field equations • Scalar mediators • Historical unification approaches • Field coupling mechanisms • Experimental tests

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{Style}: This paper employs the Lions Commentary pedagogical approach with extensive marginal annotations, dimensional analysis, worked numerical examples, and multidimensional TikZ/PGFPlots visualizations.
\end{abstract}
