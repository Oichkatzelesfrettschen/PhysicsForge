\begin{equation}
  E_{\mathrm{ZPE}}^{\mathrm{GEM}} = \int \rho_{\mathrm{vac}}(\mathbf{x}) \phi(\mathbf{x}) \left[1 + \beta \frac{B_g^2(\mathbf{x})}{B_{\mathrm{crit}}^2}\right] d^3x
  \eqtag{G}{ZPE}{GEM}
  \label{eq:pais:zpe-gem-interaction}
\end{equation}

\noindent\textbf{Physical Interpretation:} This equation describes self-sustaining energy extraction through ZPE-GEM integration. The scalar field $\phi$ couples to vacuum energy density $\rho_{\mathrm{vac}}$, modulated by gravitomagnetic field strength $B_g$. The critical field $B_{\mathrm{crit}} = c^2/(G r_P) \sim 10^{52}$ s$^{-1}$ represents the Planck-scale gravitomagnetic field strength.

\noindent\textbf{Enhancement Mechanism:} The factor $[1 + \beta B_g^2/B_{\mathrm{crit}}^2]$ enables resonant amplification when gravitomagnetic fields approach critical values. For Earth's field $B_g \sim 10^{-14}$ s$^{-1}$, the enhancement is negligible: $B_g^2/B_{\mathrm{crit}}^2 \sim 10^{-132}$. However, near neutron stars ($B_g \sim 10^{20}$ s$^{-1}$), the effect becomes measurable: $B_g^2/B_{\mathrm{crit}}^2 \sim 10^{-64}$.

\noindent\textbf{Coherence Requirement:} The integral is dominated by regions where $\phi$ and $\rho_{\mathrm{vac}}$ maintain phase coherence over scales $\lambda \sim \hbar/(m_{\phi} c)$. Decoherence from thermal or quantum noise suppresses the effective interaction strength by factor $\exp(-\Gamma t)$ where $\Gamma$ is the decoherence rate.

\noindent\textbf{Connection to Aether Framework:} This energy functional matches the Aether scalar-ZPE coupling (Eq.~\eqref{eq:aether:scalar-zpe-energy}) with the addition of gravitomagnetic modulation, providing the bridge between Pais and Aether models.
