\begin{equation}
  E_{\text{qp}}(k) = \sqrt{\Delta^2 + v_F^2(k - k_F)^2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{\alpha_n}{k^n} \cos(nk d_{\text{node}})
  \eqtag{X}{QM}{T}
  \label{eq:genesis:quasiparticle-excitation}
\end{equation}
% Notes: Dispersion relation for quasiparticle excitations in the Genesis nodespace.
% The first term represents the gapped spectrum with gap $\Delta$ and Fermi velocity $v_F$
% at Fermi momentum $k_F$, analogous to BCS-type excitations. The sum captures
% lattice effects from the discrete nodespace structure with inter-node distance $d_{\text{node}}$
% and coupling coefficients $\alpha_n$. These quasiparticles emerge as collective
% excitations of the nodespace vacuum and carry quantum numbers corresponding to
% symmetries of the underlying network. The spectrum exhibits both gapped and gapless
% phases depending on the nodespace connectivity and can support topological modes.
