\section{Phase 6: Validation Strategy (Summarised)}

Phase~6 reframes Genesis as an empirically testable programme. Core elements include:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Fractal energy distributions:} search for scale-invariant signatures in cosmological and laboratory data.
  \item \textbf{Nodespace resonances:} monitor gravitational and electromagnetic observables for modular tunnelling patterns.
  \item \textbf{Origami fold detection:} explore interferometric probes for dimensional folding effects.
\end{enumerate}

\paragraph{Roadmap.}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Prioritise experiments that overlap with Aether and Pais predictions to maximise cross-validation.
  \item Define quantitative metrics (signal-to-noise, confidence intervals) for each proposed observation.
  \item Establish data pipelines linking raw measurements to the LaTeX synthesis (tables/figures).
\end{itemize}
